Frequently Asked Questions

  1. I am unable to sync my app data?

    Check your internet connection to ensure that is working fine. If the problem still persists, contact your Admin.

  2. My app keeps crashing?

    Click 3 vertical dots showing in upper right-side to access Settings page. From the HELP & SUPPORT section, tap on Send debug report. Your Admin will use this report to analyze the issue and suggest the solution.

  3. How can I change my login PIN?

    Click 3 vertical dots showing in upper right-side to access Settings page. From the SECURITY section, tap on Setup/Change PIN to recreate your PIN.

  4. How do I remove blocked apps from my app catalog?

    Click 3 vertical dots showing in upper right-side to access Settings page. From the APP SETTINGS section, tap on Hide blocked apps.

  5. My apps are not updating?

    Check your internet connection to ensure that is working fine If the problem still persists, contact your Admin.
