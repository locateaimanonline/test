Deleting Enrollment Rules

About this task

To delete an enrollment rule:

Procedure

  1. Open the Devices view and select Manage > Enrollment Rules.
  2. On the Enrollment Rules view, open More > Delete corresponding with the enrollment rule you want to delete.
  3. Click Delete.

Results

The deleted enrollment rule will no longer appear in the Enrollment Rules view.
