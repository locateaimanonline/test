Viewing the History of a Shareable Link

About this task

To view the history of a shareable link:

Procedure

  1. In the Apps view, click Shareable Links.
  2. Beside the link whose history you want to see, click > History.

Results

In the Shareable Links History view, the account name (the channel account name of the user who imported the app) and the date and time it was imported are displayed.
