Getting Started

Getting started with SOTI Snap is simple, fast, and easy. As a cloud application, setup is minimal, you can access it from anywhere and you'll always receive the latest SOTI Snap features immediately.

  1. Contact SOTI Sales to purchase a SOTI Snap license or download a trial version from the SOTI ONE Downloads page.

  2. Open a browser and log into your SOTI Snap console using the URL and credentials provided by your SOTI account representative

The SOTI Snap console is split into four main sections, or views: Dashboard, Apps, Devices, and Settings. Move between views by click their respective tabs from the left panel. Whenever you log into the SOTI Snap console, you'll land on the Dashboard view, which provides at a glance information on your SOTI Snap environment.

Learn more about each view at Navigating the SOTI Snap Console.

Once you've configured the SOTI Snap console to your preferences (added users, set console security, and so on), move on to Building Apps!

Tip: Open the Avatar dropdown menu in the upper right and select Video Tutorials to view several quick tutorials on how to use SOTI Snap.
